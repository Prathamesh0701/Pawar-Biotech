  • Agriculture,
  • Distillery,
  • Sugar,
  • Waste Water Treatment,
  • Food,
  • Animal Health
Pawar Biotech continues to set trends with the research and development of new applications for the use of Organic and Enzymes based Products across various various Industry, Distillery and Agricultural sectors
Our aim is to replace chemicals products with Eco-Friendly Organic and Enzyme based Products and to make healthy environment for crops to grow and give healthy fruits or yield
