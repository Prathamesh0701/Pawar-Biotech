ABOUT US:-

Almost 23 years of experience have taken PAWAR BIO-TECH from strength to strength in the agriculture business.
Certified manufacturing units of PAWAR BIO-TECH has built up a reputation for excellence of foundations of consistently high quality, constantly expanding product portfolio and timely deliveries.
PAWAR BIO-TECH is the prominent processors and suppliers of quality range of Organic manure (city compost), organic granules, micronutrients, growth promoting hormones, organic viricide, Bio Pesticides, Bio Fungicide, Soil Conditioners, sprayer, banana tissue culture.

Our Commitments:

PAWAR BIO-TECH strives to improve agriculture globally and bring good farming through effective and high-quality products, offering competitive prices.
We are focus on building a world class company with national and international reach through our commitment to customer satisfaction, comprehensive product range, unflagging attention on quality and by building mutually beneficial relationships.

Large Scale Production:

Huge production capacities ensure the products availability at short notice and at competitive prices, enabling PAWAR BIO-TECH to cater to government as well as private sectors.
Our goal is to bring solace to suffering huminity world over through high quality fertilizers at most economic prices.

  • Maintaining eco-friendly production
