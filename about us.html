ABOUT US:-

Almost 23 years of experience have taken PAWAR BIO-TECH from strength to strength in the agriculture business. Certified manufacturing units of PAWAR BIO-TECH has built up a reputation for excellence of foundations of consistently high quality, constantly expanding product portfolio and timely deliveries. PAWAR BIO-TECH is the prominent processors and suppliers of quality range of Enzmyme based products, Organic manure (city compost), organic granules, micronutrients, growth promoting hormones, organic viricide, Bio Pesticides, Bio Fungicide, Soil Conditioners waste water management, animal products, sprayer, banana tissue culture, polyhouse structures, etc.
