SOIL BOOST

The Secret Behind Fertile Soil

Description:

Soil Boost is a highly effective organic constituent that decreases the unwanted Minerals/Salts in the barren and hard soil and thus maintains the pH level and increases the fertility of the soil.

Why Soil Boost vital?

Friends, nowadays by using more fertilizers to obtain higher yield or by using unwanted fertilizers we make soil/ land infertile day by day. By doing so salt present in the fertilizer inform of filler materials accumulated in the first layer of soil and form a smear of salts. Same ways flood irrigation will also increase the metal in soil and make it hard and compact.

Benefits:

  • Soil Boost increases the texture and structure of soil.
  • It improves the PH level of soils.
  • It dissolves the all types of carbonate & bicarbonate salts which is deposited in first layers of soils.
  • It makes the soil porous and increase C:N ratio
  • It enhances the number of rots and root hair tremendously.
  • Plants, trees will absorb more water, P&K which is present in soil and root hairs expands downwards to the soil.
  • It will also check the fungal & nematodes problem effectively.
  • Remove of metal smear we can archive uniform rapid growth, germination & development of all craps.
  • It can reduce the cost of fertilizers.
  • It's non-hazardous and very easy to handle.

Work's on:

  • Provides immunity to the plant and thus given uniform crop growth. It provides strength to each type of soils and helps in germination, growth and development of all season of crops because growth cannot wait.

Dose:-

Drip system:

  • 500 ml/1 Acre
  • 1000 ml/1 Ha

For Soil:

  • 500ml/1 Bigha
  • 1000 ml/ 1 Acre

Methodology:

1. Drip Systems:

  • Apply 500ml Drip Soil Boost in 100 Litres water and stop the drip system after 10 minutes until the solution will go into the pipes.
  • Restart the drip system after half hour it if chock up, Jam is less than 50%
  • Restart the drip system after 1 to 2 Hrs if chock up/ Jam is more that 50%

2. For Soil:

  • It is use for soil metal Removal from the first layer of soils.
  • Mix drip Soil Boost with 10 kg Urea Sand and just broadcast it with free hands.
